1. Required Documentation

  • Vehicle Registration Certificate
  • Export License
  • Certificate of Origin
  • Purchase Invoice
  • Vehicle Inspection Certificate
  • Shipping Insurance Documents

2. Vehicle Inspection Requirements

All vehicles exported from China must pass a comprehensive inspection covering:

  • Mechanical Condition
  • Emissions Standards
  • Safety Features
  • Vehicle History Verification
  • Odometer Reading Certification

3. Shipping Methods

Two primary shipping methods are available:

Container Shipping

Most economical for multiple vehicles. Options include:

  • 20ft container (1 vehicle)
  • 40ft container (2-3 vehicles)
  • 40ft high cube (2-3 vehicles with extra height)

RO-RO (Roll-On/Roll-Off)

Ideal for single vehicle transport. Benefits include:

  • Lower packaging requirements
  • Faster loading/unloading
  • More flexible scheduling

4. Customs Clearance

Key steps in the customs clearance process:

  • Filing of Export Declaration
  • Customs Inspection
  • Payment of Export Duties (if applicable)
  • Obtaining Customs Clearance Certificate
Important Note: Customs requirements vary by destination country. We recommend verifying specific import requirements for your target market before proceeding with the export process.

5. Costs to Consider

  • Export Documentation: $200-500
  • Container Shipping: $2,000-4,000
  • Insurance: 1-3% of vehicle value
  • Customs Duties: Varies by country
  • Inspection Fees: $100-300

6. Timeline

Typical export process timeline:

  • Documentation Preparation: 3-5 days
  • Vehicle Inspection: 1-2 days
  • Customs Clearance: 2-3 days
  • Shipping Time: 15-45 days (destination dependent)
